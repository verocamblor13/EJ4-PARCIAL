Verónica Camblor

Integral Designer exploring the intersection between visual culture, technology, and new ways of thinking.

About Me

I am a design student focused on understanding how images, objects, and systems shape the way we perceive and interact with the world.

My work moves between visual exploration, conceptual thinking, and material experimentation, seeking to create projects that question what we see and how we interpret it.

Work & Approach

I am particularly interested in the relationship between design and contemporary visual culture, exploring how digital media, perception, and representation redefine reality.

Through projects ranging from product design to visual systems, I aim to develop ideas that are both analytical and expressive, combining functionality with a strong conceptual foundation.
